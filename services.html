Services

Nick Uren Law provides strategic legal and tax advisory services for businesses and individuals facing complex domestic and international issues.

Business Formation

Advising founders and business owners on entity selection, organizational structuring, and early-stage planning to support long-term operational and tax efficiency.

Business Planning

Providing practical legal and tax guidance for growing businesses, including operational structuring, ownership considerations, and strategic planning issues.

Tax Planning

Developing tax-efficient strategies tailored to businesses, owners, and individuals, with attention to both immediate concerns and long-term objectives.

Inbound Tax Planning

Advising foreign businesses and investors entering the United States on structuring, tax exposure, compliance, and operational considerations.

International Tax Planning

Assisting clients with cross-border tax matters involving foreign entities, global operations, international reporting, and multijurisdictional planning.

Estate Planning

Supporting wealth planning strategies that address domestic and international considerations, business succession, and long-term asset preservation goals.

Tax Audits

Representing clients in tax audit matters with a focus on technical analysis, efficient responses, and practical management of risk and exposure.

Tax Controversy

Advising clients through disputes with tax authorities, including administrative controversy matters, strategic responses, and resolution planning.

Mergers and Acquisitions

Providing transaction support on structuring, diligence, tax considerations, and planning issues affecting buyers, sellers, and business owners.
