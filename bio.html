Bio

Nick Uren is an international business and tax attorney who advises businesses and individuals on tax planning, business structuring, international compliance, and related cross-border matters. His practice is designed to provide practical legal and tax guidance to clients navigating complex domestic and international issues.

Nick’s background includes experience with Ernst & Young, Deloitte, Grant Thornton, and Aldrich Advisors. That experience informs an advisory approach grounded in both technical depth and practical business judgment.

Professional Background

Nick’s work has focused on international tax law, international business law, international compliance, international estate planning, tax planning, Oregon tax law, Washington tax law, and mergers and acquisitions. He advises on issues ranging from early-stage planning and business formation to tax controversy and strategic transactions.
