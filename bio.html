Bio

Nick Uren advises businesses and individuals on international tax planning, business structuring, compliance, controversy, and related strategic matters.

Professional Profile

International Business and Tax Attorney

Nick Uren brings a practical, business-oriented perspective to complex tax and legal issues, with experience spanning international matters, planning, compliance, and transaction-related advisory work.

Background

Nick’s background includes experience with Ernst & Young, Deloitte, Grant Thornton, and Aldrich Advisors. That experience informs an advisory approach grounded in technical depth, sound judgment, and commercial practicality.

His work has included international tax law, international business law, international compliance, international estate planning, tax planning, Oregon tax law, Washington tax law, and mergers and acquisitions.
